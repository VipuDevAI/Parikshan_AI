7. TIMETABLE SYSTEM (VERY STRICT)
------------------------------------------------
Timetable & Substitution are ONE engine.

When entering timetable:
Ask:
• Wing-wise OR Whole-school

Authority:
• Whole school → Principal
• Wing-wise → Wing Admin

Mandatory inputs before generation:
• Teacher ↔ Subject ↔ Class ↔ Section mapping
• Class teachership (configurable by wing admin)
• Periods per week (PPW)
• Max periods per day (MPPD)
• Lunch period
• After-lunch light periods
• Lab periods per week
• II & III language rules
• Senior secondary streams
• Shared teachers across wings

STRICTLY FOLLOW THIS GENERATION ORDER:
1. Initialize trackers
2. Assign class teacher periods first
3. Assign II & III language (combined & split)
4. Assign lab sessions (consecutive)
5. Fill remaining PPW
6. Fill FREE
7. Maintain LOGS
8. Freeze MASTER TIMETABLE

------------------------------------------------
8. SUBSTITUTION SYSTEM (STRICT)
------------------------------------------------
Substitution MUST read ONLY from MASTER TIMETABLE.

Leave types:
• Full day
• Half day
• Permission (period-wise)
• On-duty
• Incharge-assigned duty (1 or more periods)

Leave submission:
• Before 6:45 AM
• Approved by Principal

Duty assignment:
• Assigned by Principal / Senior / Wing Admin
• Period-level unavailability
• Behaves like partial leave

STRICT substitution logic:
• Identify free teachers period-wise
• Exclude:
  – Overloaded (7+ periods)
  – On leave
  – Permanent exclusions
• Max 3 substitutions per teacher per day
• Scoring:
  + Subject match
  + Class match
  + Lower workload
  + Period gap
• Avoid continuous periods
• If none available → log

Maintain:
• Substitution log
• Audit trail
• Daily substitution report
